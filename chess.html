Knight-Mare

Chess is a board game for two players, called White and Black, each controlling an army of chess pieces in their color, with the objective to checkmate the opponent's king. There are 6 Types of pieces in total.They are listed with their powers below

PAWN:

A Pawn is the weakest member in the chess holding 1 point. It only moves in forward direction. At beggining a pawn can move 2 steps ahead and further continue with 1 step each. A Pawn has an ability called "PROMOTION". Once a Pawn reaches to end of the either end of the board it can promote itself to a Queen, bishop, knight or a rook

BISHOP:

A Bishop is the piece in chess which can move diagonally from one end to another end of board. A kingdom has 2 Bishops which includes a black and a white bishop It holds 3 Points

KNIGHT:

A Knight is the trickiest piece in this game. It also holds 3 points. It moves in "L" shape. either front, back, right, left. A Knight can fork pieces which gains any piece at a move.

ROOK:

A Rook is a Straight-forward, Powerful, 5 pointed piece. It moves straight in either directions of the board. A Singe Rook can Mate opponent's king

QUEEN:

The powerful piece in chess is Queen. It holds 9 points. A Queen can move Straightly, Diagonally, back and forth.

KING:

The Main Piece in the game is King. It holds infinite points. A King can't be captured by any piece, but can check on all other possible moves of king called checkmate. A King can move only one step in any direction
